About Me

I'm a third-year Geoinformation Engineering student at FST Tangier with a strong foundation in both geospatial science and full-stack software development. I combine hands-on experience in GIS and remote sensing with practical development skills to build spatial applications that solve real operational problems.

Driven by applied research and end-user impact, I've delivered projects ranging from temporal land-cover analysis to production GIS tools and mobile routing apps. I'm comfortable working across the stack — from geodata acquisition and preprocessing through spatial analysis to designing web and mobile front ends that make geospatial insights actionable.

I'm particularly experienced in map-based interfaces, spatial databases (PostGIS), and automated geoprocessing workflows. My technical toolkit spans from QGIS and ArcGIS to modern web frameworks like NestJS, Angular, and React, allowing me to bridge the gap between geospatial data and user-friendly applications.
